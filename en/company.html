ABOUT US

Company Profile

Darton Industrial Co., Ltd. was founded in Taoyuan, Taiwan in 1983. Since its establishment, it has been a creative designer and manufacturer of high-quality refractory materials. In 2004, we passed ISO 9001: 2000 certification.

Accumulated more than 30 Years of experience in producing special refractory materials. Based on the accumulation of past experience, Darton continues to improve production technology and product R&D and design, providing the best functional refractory materials in the market to meet the constantly updated needs of customers, and bringing maximum benefits to customers through the application of quality advantages and customer-oriented strategies. Now Darton Industrial Co., Ltd. has become one of the world's most important suppliers of special refractory materials.

Our goal is to continue to produce high-quality, high-reliability, high-value products and to help customers improve product quality, increase production capacity, save energy, and reduce costs when using our products.
